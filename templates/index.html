Do Philly students have the books they need?

In Philadelphia, there’s been a recurring complaint that students don’t have the books they need. But it’s been hard to prove. This year, with severe budget cuts looming and limited for new materials, keeping track of these materials is more important than ever. This project examines the data to investigate the answer. Do our public school children have the learning materials they need?
