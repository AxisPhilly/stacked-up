What's here?

Below are inventory records about reading and math programs at {{ school.name.title }}. Depending upon the quality of school’s own records, it may or may not show what the school actually has. For each subject, we display any curricula that the school appears to have materials for.

For each curriculum, we display the materials that students in this classroom are likely to need, based upon what that curriculum includes. If you see "no records" as the quantity, that means this material is typically used in this curriculum, but the school doesn’t appear to have it.

How to use this tool

For each curriculum, you'll see the materials students need. If you care about this school, you can calculate the cost of missing materials by changing the number in the “materials needed” column.

The dollar figure you see at the end of each curriculum is an estimate of that cost, based on the District’s negotiated prices.
